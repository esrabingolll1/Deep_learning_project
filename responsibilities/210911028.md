## 4) Results
| Experiment | Best Test Acc (%) | Final Test Acc (%) | Epoch to Reach 70% |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Cosine \| Base (No Aug, No Smooth) | 74.42 | 74.42 | 3 |
| Cosine \| Aug+Smooth | **79.43** | **79.43** | 3 |
| Plateau \| Base (No Aug, No Smooth) | 75.72 | 75.16 | 3 |
| Plateau \| Aug+Smooth | 78.92 | 78.92 | 4 |

## 5) Technical Interpretation
- Augmentation + label smoothing improves robustness of validation behavior and reduces over-confidence.
- Cosine scheduling provided the best final performance in this experiment set.
- Plateau remained competitive, especially for stabilization when validation loss saturates.
